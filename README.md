## About me

I’m a Computer Science student at the **University of Toronto Mississauga** with experience across AI engineering, software development, product development, and enterprise applications.

I like finding the shape inside complicated problems—then building systems that make those problems feel useful, intuitive, and a little less intimidating. I care about technical depth, but I also care about how a product feels in someone’s hands.
